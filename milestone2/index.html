Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

Information regarding our small start-up business, such as the relevant competition nearby, as well as the intended audience!

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Much of it will be self produced, including images and text. However, certain images may be borrowed from copyright free sources on the internet.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Because it will save each user time who decides to choose our company by allowing them to have their oil changes completed from home, as opposed to needing to travel to reach somewhere.

Why should a site be built for this?

This site is intended to spread information, specifically about the company and its future developments. Having a site will make it easier for users to navigate the various services provided, and be able to quickly reach out to us.

What would you like users to do at your site?

Discover the multitude of benefits of choosing our company oil changes, as well as learn more about who we are and what we do. We would like for users to contact us if what is on the website is of interest to them.

What is the goal of the site?

To promote a small start up business and to improve the possibilities of advertising in the future. It will also serve to simplify the experience of the client by allowing them to quickly and simply navigate to where they want or need to go.
